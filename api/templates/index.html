Smart Attendance & Bunk Planner for PSG College of Technology

Bunker is a specialized utility designed for students of PSG College of Technology (PSG Tech) and PSG Institute of Advanced Studies (PSG IAS). It simplifies the process of checking academic attendance logs by providing a direct sync with the official eCampus login portal.

Why Use Bunker?

  • eCampus Portal Integration: Securely log in using your student roll number and eCampus password to instantly retrieve your official attendance register.
  • 75 Percent Rule Compliance: Determine exactly how many classes you can safely miss (bunk) or how many consecutive classes you must attend to maintain the mandatory 75% attendance threshold.
  • Academic Calendar & Schedule: Track holidays, CA test dates, laboratory exams, and semester end dates with a custom interactive timeline.
  • Timetable Tracker: View your daily lecture periods and course mapping codes dynamically synced from the college database.

PSG Tech Attendance Formula

Attendance is calculated on an hourly basis. Each period attended counts towards your total percentage. Bunker uses the formula: Percentage = (Hours Attended / Total Conducted Hours) * 100. The simulator allows you to test mock scenarios (e.g., medical leave exemptions) to plan your schedule without falling below 75% or 65% (the condonation boundary).
